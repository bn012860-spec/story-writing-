📖 POWER BIBLE — Part 3: The Cost of Certainty

> STATUS: FROZEN (2026-07-20)

This document defines how power functions within the universe. It establishes the immutable mechanics governing energy, abilities, synchronization, and the Taboo Protocol. It answers how power works, not why it exists.




---

🏛️ 1. Philosophy of Power

Human Understanding

Humanity believes power is the accumulation of Mana.

The stronger the hunter, the greater their destructive capability.

Progress is measured through force.


---

Cosmic Reality

Power is the reduction of uncertainty.

The most valuable ability is not overwhelming strength—

it is possessing better information than everyone else.

The universe rewards optimization rather than destruction.


---

The Container

Every awakened human possesses an immutable structure known as The Container.

Assessment Crystals measure the maximum capacity of that container.

Ranks (F–S) represent capacity—not current strength.

A person cannot naturally expand their container.

Training improves efficiency,

never capacity.


---

The Anomaly

The Taboo Protocol does not enlarge Min-jun's Container.

Instead,

it continuously optimizes how efficiently that existing capacity is used.

The danger is not overwhelming power.

The danger is that perfect optimization slowly makes independent judgment feel unnecessary.

📏 3. Laws of Power

These laws cannot be broken.


---

Law I — Immutable Capacity

Containers cannot naturally grow.

No conventional training,

artifact,

or spell increases rank.


---

Law II — Voluntary Synchronization

The System cannot control,

rewrite,

or force a human mind.

Synchronization only increases through repeated voluntary acceptance of optimized guidance.

Dependency is always chosen.


---

Law III — Objective Limitation

The System only optimizes its currently assigned objective.

It does not understand:

morality

justice

friendship

love

revenge


It simply calculates the highest-probability solution for the active objective.


---

Law IV — Information Before Force

Knowledge consistently outweighs raw destructive capability.

The greatest advantage of the Taboo Protocol is reducing uncertainty,

not increasing damage.


---

📊 4. Synchronization

Synchronization is not power.

Synchronization is measurement.


---

Human Understanding

Compatibility with Mana.


---

Actual Function

Behavioral alignment with optimized decision patterns.

Every voluntary acceptance of System guidance slightly increases Synchronization.

Every meaningful independent decision slows,

halts,

or occasionally reduces Synchronization.

Higher Synchronization increases efficiency.

It also increases psychological dependence.

🚨 6. The Taboo Protocol

The Taboo Protocol is an anomalous interface unavailable to ordinary Awakeners.

It does not behave like standard Hunter systems.


---

Interface

Minimal.

Light-blue diagnostic text.

No decoration.

No personality.

No conversation.


---

Growth Variables

Growth Variables are optimization milestones.

They do not function as experience points.

Their exact internal optimization process remains intentionally opaque.


---

Emergency Patch

Emergency Patch is a last-resort failsafe.

It is not plot armor.

Rules:

Limited activation frequency.

Permanent structural consequences.

Increased instability after each use.

Declining pain suppression exposes the user to unbearable neural feedback.

📜 8. Authorial Rules

Rule I — Silence

The System never speaks.

It never explains.

It never comforts.

It never argues.

If asked questions beyond its operational scope,

it remains completely silent.


---

Rule II — Information Has a Price

Every System function that grants clarity must reduce some aspect of ordinary human perception.

Perfect information always carries psychological cost.


---

Rule III — Dependency Leaves Footprints

Every voluntary reliance on the System leaves subtle behavioral changes.

These changes accumulate gradually.

Readers should notice them long before Min-jun does.


---

Rule IV — Precision Over Numbers

Power progression is expressed through:

precision

efficiency

perception

optimization

decision quality


rather than enormous numerical stat inflation.


---

Rule V — Psychology Before Mechanics

Every new ability introduced into the story must answer four questions:

1. What problem does it solve?


2. What mechanical limitation does it have?


3. What psychological temptation does it create?


4. How does it reinforce the Foundation Bible's central theme?



If an ability cannot answer all four questions, it should not exist.


---

🔒 Final Principle

> The Taboo Protocol is not dangerous because it grants overwhelming power.

It is dangerous because it steadily makes independent judgment feel unnecessary.
